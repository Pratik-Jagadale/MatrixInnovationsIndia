कुंभारासारखा गुरू नाही रे जगात - Kumbhara Sarkha Guru Nahi re Jagat
This demo was created for a Marathi Poem Kumbhara Sarkha Guru Nahi re Jagat (कुंभारासारखा गुरू नाही रे जगात) by Ga Di Madgulkar (Gajanan Digambar Madgulkar). The poem gives analogy between a Potter or Pot maker and a Guru i.e., Teacher. A Pot maker is able to create various types of Objects and pots by treating any Clay with gentle actions along with Harsh but necessary treatment to Mould them into strong object. Similarly, a Guru guides and nurtures disciples for their growth but also teaches rigorous lessons which will prepare disciples for future.
